REPORTING TIMELINE

3/2/2020 is the Deadline for Form Year 2020

The filing deadlines for the Affordable Care Act reporting are February 28th if filing on paper and March 31st if filing electronically. Forms 1095-C and 1095-B must be provided to employees by March 2 after the end of the year for which the information is being reported. If the deadline falls on a holiday/weekend the deadline is moved to the next working day. Reporting is on a calendar-year basis, regardless of a group's plan year or renewal date.

Deadline for the Form Year 2019:

Reporting is required for the form year 2020. Statements must be provided to full-time employees by March 2.
