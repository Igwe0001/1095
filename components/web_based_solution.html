How our online 1095 filing service works

You simply have to sign up with us using your email id and a password of your choice. We ask you some basic questions. Once you are signed up, you can start entering your information. With your account, you can file for as many companies as you want, and under each company, you can have as many employees as you need. Once you have entered your employee information you can begin filing your forms. You choose the form you are interested in and enter the information. You also have the option to upload your information if you do not want to enter this manually on our site. After you have completed your forms and paid for the service, you can sit back and relax. We take care of e-filing with the IRS and printing and mailing recipient copies.
