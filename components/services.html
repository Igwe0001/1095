TIN MATCHING

Tax ID Verification Services

It is important for you to make sure that you are reporting correct Tax ID/name combinations to the IRS when submitting 1099 forms. To prevent the IRS from levying penalties for incorrect reporting and to reduce the cost associated with issuing B-Notices and withholding federal taxes from form 1099 payments, it is highly recommended that you verify the accuracy of the recipients’ Tax IDs.
