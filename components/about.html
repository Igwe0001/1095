About Us

Our 1095 filing services

At Outsource1095.com we strive to provide an outstanding service for the information reporting industry. In the last ten years, we have grown to become the most dependable service provider for 1098, 1099, and W-2 filing. We offer a simplified and comprehensive outsourcing solution. Our customers are spread all over the United States, and we are proud to provide solutions that meet the needs of businesses and institutions of all sizes.

We offer two distinct services.

Web-based Solution: For smaller businesses, we provide a DO-IT-YOURSELF web service, where businesses can go online and fill out or upload their information. Our Service Bureau, prints and mails recipient copies and electronically files with the IRS. Please visit www.OnlineFileTaxes.com or www.Efile360.com for more information.

Outsourcing Solution: For larger businesses, we offer a comprehensive outsourcing solution. Our clients do not have to do anything other than providing us form related data that you want to file. After we receive the data, we import it into our system and make sure that everything is correctly imported. We print and mail recipient copies and electronically file with the IRS. We provide PDF copies for your records.

We also offer E-Delivery for all our forms so that recipients can choose to retrieve their statements electronically.

We keep our software updated with the latest regulations, and you do not have to worry about keeping track of ever-changing rules and guidelines.

We are an IRS-approved e-file provider.

If you have any questions or comments about our services, please feel free to contact us at any time.
