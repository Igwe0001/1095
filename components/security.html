Security

At Outsource1095.com keeping our client’s information safe and secure is our topmost priority. We provide a secure environment to maintain the confidentiality of client information. We use advanced SSL to offer you a secure website. We use Site Lock to monitor our website 24/7 for PCI Compliance. We use a highly safe environment to host our servers. All our staff members are US citizens and are well trained to maintain sensitive information securely. We do not share or sell any of our client’s data. Please review our Privacy Policy posted on our website. We have completed the SOC 2 Type I compliance audit and have passed without exception. The SOC 2 is an attestation standard put forth by the Auditing Standards Board (ASP) of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA). The attestation confirms that our security standards and system are designed to achieve stated control objectives.
